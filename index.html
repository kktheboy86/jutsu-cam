Hand Sign Guide

Match your pose to trigger each jutsu.

Kage Bunshin

Both hands close together, index + middle up, ring + pinky mostly folded.

Chidori

One palm fully open, while your other hand grips near that wrist.

Rasengan

One open palm above the other, parallel and facing each other with a gap between them.
